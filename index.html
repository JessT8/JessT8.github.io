Click the letters
Using HTML, CSS and JavaScript DOM manipulation, this is a game whereby players need to click on the moving letters according to the words displayed in sequential order before time runs out. As the level get higher, the moving letters moves at a faster rate. Players health will be deducted when clicking on a wrong letter.
Movie-log
Using express and APIs (Cloudinary and MovieDB), Movie-log enables users to view movies and keep track of them. They can favorite and mark the movies they've watched. They can also view other users' movie list after they followed them.
Petsitr

Tasty.ly
Using Ruby on Rails and React, Tasty.ly enables restaurant owners to add their restaurants and menu item for users to favorite and keep track of. Restaurant owners can also view the number of favorites for their restaurants and menu items.
